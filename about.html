About

Visual Scrape gives you the information you want, when you want it, in an easy-to-digest format. Visual Scrape takes images from the internet pertaining to a user's inputted keyword,and displays them based on a procedurally-created list. Users have the ability to rate images and influence their placement for when the keyword is searched for again.

Visual Scrape was made over the course of 36 hours at Electric City Hacks, thanks to the combined efforts of Prashant Patel, Ousmane Amadou, Kohilan Mohanarajan, and Joseph Ugiomoh.
